Our Story

Three generations
of dough and patience

It started in 1987 when Nonna Elena set up a small oven in the back of her grocery, baking just enough bread for the neighborhood. Word spread. The oven got bigger. The grocery turned into a bakery.

Today, her grandkids run the place — same recipes, same long fermentations, same insistence on doing things the slow way because that's the only way it's worth doing.
