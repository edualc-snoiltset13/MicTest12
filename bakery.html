Our Story

Since 2008, Golden Crust has been the heart of the neighborhood — a place where the smell of warm bread greets you at the door. We mill our own flour, ferment our dough slowly, and never use shortcuts. Every loaf and pastry is a little piece of craft, made by hands that care.
