Our Story

Golden Crust began in 2010 as a tiny neighborhood oven with a simple belief: great bread brings people together. Three generations of bakers later, we still start every day before sunrise, kneading by hand and baking in our stone hearth.

Every loaf, pastry, and cake is made fresh daily — no preservatives, no shortcuts, just real ingredients and a whole lot of patience.
