III. Why It's Happening

Made by Us.

01

Fossil Fuels

Burning coal, oil, and gas for power, transport, and heat is the single largest source of global emissions — over 75% of greenhouse gases.

02

Deforestation

Forests absorb CO₂. Cleared for cattle, soy, and palm, they release stored carbon — and the planet loses its lungs.

03

Agriculture

Livestock emit methane — 80x more potent than CO₂ over 20 years. Fertilizers add nitrous oxide. Food systems drive ~25% of emissions.

04

Industry

Cement, steel, and chemicals are carbon-intensive. Industrial processes account for roughly a quarter of global emissions.
