About Théod

Clothing made to be kept, not replaced.

Our philosophy

Less, but better

Théod was founded on a simple idea: the world doesn't need more clothes — it needs better ones. We release small, considered collections built around versatile silhouettes that move easily between seasons and settings.

We'd rather sell you one coat you wear for a decade than ten you wear for a season.

What we stand for

Our values

🍃

Responsible materials

Organic cotton, merino wool, and certified low-impact fabrics.

⚒

Built to last

Reinforced seams and quality construction in every piece.

🤝

Fair production

Partner workshops with safe conditions and fair wages.

💰

Honest pricing

Direct-to-you, without the traditional retail markup.

Where it started

From a small studio

What began as a two-person studio sketching patterns on a kitchen table is now a small team obsessed with the details — the weight of a fabric, the drape of a hem, the way a collar sits after fifty washes.

We're independent and intend to stay that way, answering to the people who wear our clothes rather than to a quarterly trend cycle.
