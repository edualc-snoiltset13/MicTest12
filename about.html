Our Story

A little neighborhood bakery built on patience, good flour, and the smell of warm bread.

Baked with Care Since 2008

Golden Crust began with a single oven and a simple belief: good bread takes time. Since 2008, we've been the heart of the neighborhood — a place where the smell of warm bread greets you at the door each morning.

We mill our own flour, ferment our dough slowly, and never use shortcuts. Every loaf and pastry is a little piece of craft, made by hands that care.

From our sourdough's 24-hour ferment to buttery croissants folded by hand, we bake everything fresh in small batches throughout the day — so what you take home is always at its best.
